Our portfolio

An exciting village

Our agency has found a great architecture team and made a contract with them. We have agreed about 10% discount and that’s great, cause the initial cost of the village was 700 000$, and after a discount it became 630 000$. We have ed all the entire accounting. We’ve finished this village in 4 weeks, and the client has bought a medium package with 6 months support. The client was fully satisfied, and you can see his feedback in the “Rates” section.

Tracy’s plan

The “Tracy’s Plan” is a project where our client needed to create a strategy for his business and also to create a business plan. Our plan-director produced a great plan and consulted our client about where to find partners and investors. The client has bought a medium package with 6 months support and our plan-director and managers are still supporting his projects, giving advices and find partners and investors.

Gregories garden

“Gregories garden” project is all about interesting knoledges , great wins and solutions, and the most important - great result. Client’s idea of the garden was definetly not as others and at first it was hard to find the company, which could complete the task. But then the young company has come and said, that they can solve the client’s problem. After 3 weeks of the hard work the garden was ready. Client and his family were absolutely happy.

Wonderful wedding

Marta and Glen has come to us and said: “We need a wonderful wedding, can you do it?”. It was our first wedding organization but we were fun and calm with that. There were a lot of components, which were need to be solved. Especially cake, car, guests, dress and suits, everything was on us. But we did it and the most important, Marta and Glen were really happy, so we were too. That’s what we do, make other’s life easier and happier.

Sam Smith’s clip

One day Sam Smith has come to us and asked to create a great clip for his new song “Dancing with a stranger”. We’ve already worked with a video clips so it shouldn’t be a big problem. But Sam was asking for something special, so it took some time to find a good producer and to choose a good variant. But in the end the product was great and Sam liked it. Now you can see it on YouTube, just write “Sam Smith - Dancing with a stranger”.
